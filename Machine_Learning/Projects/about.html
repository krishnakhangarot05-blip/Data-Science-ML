About Laptop Price Predictor

The Laptop Price Predictor is a machine learning-powered web application designed to estimate the market price of laptops based on their hardware specifications and user feedback data. The system evaluates important features such as brand, processor type, RAM, storage configuration, operating system, warranty, and ratings.

Using regression-based machine learning algorithms trained on real laptop datasets, the model learns pricing patterns and predicts accurate laptop prices instantly. This helps users understand the approximate value of a laptop before purchasing or comparing different configurations.

Our objective is to provide a fast, intelligent, and user-friendly platform that simplifies laptop price estimation through data-driven insights. The application demonstrates how machine learning can support smarter technology purchasing decisions and market analysis.
